Purpose and Scope

·         This policy outlines the framework for recovering Information Technology (IT) infrastructure and services swiftly and efficiently in the event of a disaster or disruptive incident.

·         The policy aims to meet a set Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and encompasses all resources and processes required for data and service restoration.

·         It covers all aspects of information security within the domain of business continuity management.

Background

·         This policy defines the organization's overarching strategy for disaster recovery.

·         The strategy entails defining the RTO, which stipulates the timeframe and service levels for restoring critical business processes post-disaster.

·         The policy outlines the procedures, responsibilities, and technical guidance to fulfill the RTO. Additionally, the policy provides contact details for personnel and service providers relevant to disaster recovery.
