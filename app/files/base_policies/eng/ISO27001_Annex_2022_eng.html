ISO 27001

路         Established by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), it represents a comprehensive security framework. This framework serves as a guide for organizations to protect the credibility, integrity and availability of their invaluable information. Containing a fourth domain, the ISO 27001 framework addresses a wide range of security aspects, including:

o   Risk Management

o   Access Control

o   Physical Security

o   Business Continuity

o   Cryptography

路         The principal objective of the ISO 27001 framework is to aid organizations in recognizing, evaluating, and effectively managing risks associated with their information assets. It further furnishes directives for formulating and executing robust security controls. Embracing the ISO 27001 framework empowers organizations to assure the safeguarding and fortification of their information.

路         In summary, the HIPAA security framework and ISO 27001 framework each bear significance for entities entrusted with Protected Health Information (PHI) or sensitive data. While the HIPAA security framework charts a course for safeguarding individual privacy, the ISO 27001 framework charts a parallel course for bolstering the security of information assets. Adhering to these guiding principles empowers organizations to uphold the sanctity and assurance of their information.
