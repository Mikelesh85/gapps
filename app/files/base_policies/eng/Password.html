Purpose and Scope

·         The Password Management Policy outlines the procedure for the secure selection and handling of passwords.

·         This policy pertains to all employees, contractors, and other personnel possessing an account on any system located at a company facility or having access to the company network.

Policy

·         Password Rotation Requirements

o   System-level passwords must undergo rotation on a quarterly basis, while user-level passwords must be rotated every six months, at a minimum.

o   In the event of suspected password compromise, the affected password must be immediately rotated, and the Engineering/Security team should be promptly notified.

·         Protection of Passwords

o   All passwords are considered confidential information and should not be shared with any individuals.

o   If requested to share a password, decline the request and consult the system owner for assistance in creating an individual user account.

o   Writing down passwords, storing them in emails, electronic notes, or mobile devices, and sharing them over the phone are strictly prohibited.

o   If electronic password storage is necessary, it must be done using an IT-approved password manager.

o   If password sharing is indispensable, use a designated password manager or grant access through a single sign-on provider.

o   The utilization of the "Remember Password" feature in applications and web browsers is discouraged.

o   In the event of suspected password compromise, promptly change the password and inform the engineering/security team.

·         Enforcement

o   Any employee or contractor found to have violated this policy may be subject to disciplinary measures.
