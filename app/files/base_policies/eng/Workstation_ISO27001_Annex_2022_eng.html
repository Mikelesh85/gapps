Purpose and Scope

·         This policy establishes a framework of optimal practices aimed at minimizing the potential for data loss or exposure through workstations.

·         The ambit of this policy encompasses all employees and contractors.

·         Workstations are herein defined as the amalgamation of company-owned and personal devices harboring company data.

Policy

·         Workstation devices are requisite to fulfill the subsequent criteria:

o   The operating system must not be more than one generation behind the current version.

o   Devices must be encrypted while at rest.

o   Devices must be locked when not in use or when the employee vacates the workstation.

o   Workstations are to be employed exclusively for authorized business functions.

o   Prompt reporting is mandatory in the event of device loss or destruction.

o   Laptops and desktop devices are to be equipped with the latest IT-approved version of antivirus software.

·          Desktop & Laptop Devices

o   The company will supply employees with either a desktop, a laptop, or a combination thereof, contingent upon their job responsibilities.

o   Contractors are responsible for furnishing their own laptops.

o   Desktops and laptops must run on macOS or Windows operating systems.

·         Mobile Devices

o   The operation of mobile devices is to adhere to the protocols outlined in the Removable Media Policy, Cloud Storage, and Bring Your Own Device Policy.

o   Mobile devices must be functional on iOS or Android platforms.

o   Company data access is exclusively permissible on mobile devices through Slack and Gmail applications.

·         Removable Media

o   The utilization of removable media must align with the directives delineated in the Removable Media Policy, Cloud Storage, and Bring Your Own Device Policy.

o   Removable media may be employed on approved devices, provided such usage remains congruous with other pertinent policies.
