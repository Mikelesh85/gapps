Purpose and Scope

·         The purpose of this policy is to establish clear and comprehensive procedures for the efficient onboarding and offboarding of users within the technical infrastructure, aimed at minimizing the risks associated with information loss or unauthorized exposure.

·         This policy is applicable to the entire spectrum of technical infrastructure components deployed across the organization.

·         The policy encompasses all personnel categories, including both full-time and part-time employees, as well as contractors.

 

Background

 

·         To effectively mitigate the risks of information loss or exposure from both internal and external sources, the organization adheres to the fundamental principle of least privilege.

·         The process of account creation and permission assignment is meticulously confined to the specific resources essential for the performance of each individual's job responsibilities.

·         In alignment with this principle, adjustments to user accounts and permissions are promptly implemented upon changes in an individual's role within the organization.

·         Upon the cessation of an individual's association with the organization, their access rights are promptly and comprehensively revoked.

 

Policy

·         User Onboarding

o   The Hiring Manager initiates communication with the Human Resources (HR) department upon the recruitment of a new employee.

o   HR communicates the new hire's particulars and designated role to the Information Technology (IT) department via email.

o   IT collaborates with pertinent stakeholders to formulate a comprehensive checklist outlining the requisite accounts and permission levels for the designated role.

o   The accountable owner of each relevant resource meticulously evaluates and authorizes the creation of the user account and the associated permissions.

o   IT, in close cooperation with the respective resource owner, configures the user's access to the requisite resources.

·         User Offboarding

o   The Hiring Manager promptly informs HR of an employee's termination.

o   HR consistently generates a weekly email report enumerating the list of terminated users and instructs IT to undertake the necessary access revocation.

o   IT ensures that access is expeditiously and effectively terminated within a maximum of five business days from the receipt of the aforementioned notification.

·         Role Transition within the Organization

o   The Hiring Manager notifies HR of any role changes within the organization.

o   HR and IT jointly adhere to the same procedural sequence outlined in the user onboarding and offboarding processes to facilitate a seamless transition.

·         Regular Review of Accounts and Permissions On a monthly basis:

o   IT and HR collaboratively review the precision of accounts and permission levels.

o   Necessary refinements are promptly effected to maintain alignment with the principle of least privilege.
