Purpose and Scope

·         This policy serves to establish comprehensive security procedures within the organization's data centers and secure equipment areas, both physical and logical.

·         It applies to all cloud-hosted providers and facilities designated as data centers or secure equipment areas, explicitly outlined within this document.

·         All management, employees, and suppliers conducting operations within these areas are subject to this policy.

Background

·         This policy delineates the governance of data centers and secure equipment areas from physical and logical security standpoints.

·         It encompasses a comprehensive inventory of data centers and secure areas utilized by the organization, specifies access control measures, outlines visitor and third-party access protocols, and mandates regular safety and security assessments.

Policy

·         The subsequent sections outline the organization's approach to data center and secure equipment area security:

·         Classification of Secure Areas: The organization designates the following areas as secure under the purview of this policy: [List all data center locations and secure areas under the organization's jurisdiction]

·         Area Management: Each data center and secure area shall be overseen by an appointed manager whose name is documented in organizational records. For on-premises data centers, the manager's name shall also be visibly posted within and near the secure area.

·         Area Marking and Access Control: Secure areas must be clearly demarcated and access shall be controlled via a locked door at minimum. A visitor access log shall be prominently positioned and easily accessible just inside the entrance.

·         Visitor Access and Protocol:

o   Individuals not employed by the organization are considered visitors.

o   Visitor access to secure areas shall adhere to the guidelines stipulated in reference [a].

o   Visitors are only permitted entry and must be accompanied by a designated employee for the duration of their stay.

o   The accompanying employee must be present throughout the visitor's time within the secure area.

o   Entry and exit times must be diligently logged in the visitor access log.

·         Prohibited Activities: Certain activities are strictly prohibited within secure areas, including:

o   Capturing photographs, videos, or audio recordings.

o   Connection of electrical devices to power sources without specific authorization.

o   Unauthorized manipulation or use of installed equipment.

o   Network device connection without explicit authorization.

o   Storage or archival of extensive paper materials.

o   Presence of flammable materials or equipment.

o   Use of portable heating devices.

o   Smoking, eating, or drinking.

·         Safety and Security Checks:

o   Regular quarterly assessments shall be conducted to ensure compliance with safety and security prerequisites within secure areas.
