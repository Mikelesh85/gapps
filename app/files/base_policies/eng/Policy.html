Purpose and Scope

  • The Policy Education and Training Policy outlines the requirements for educating employees and contractors on organizational policies.
  • This policy is applicable to all full-time employees, part-time employees, and contractors.
  • Adherence to specific policies is legally binding as stated in their Employment Offer Letter and/or Independent Contractor Agreement.

Applicability

  • Applicability
  • Upon the onboarding of a new employee or contractor, the Hiring Manager shall assess the subset of policies relevant to the individual's role.
  • The individual will be provided with five working days to review the designated policies.
  • The following details will be recorded in the Policy Training Policy Ledger:
  • Date of assignment
  • Date of completion
  • Policy title
  • Recipient
  • Assigner
  • Notes
