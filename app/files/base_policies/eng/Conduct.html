Purpose and Scope

  • The purpose of this policy is to establish clear guidelines for expected employee behavior concerning interactions with colleagues, supervisors, and the overall organization. This policy emphasizes adherence to a Code of Conduct that promotes respectful, collaborative, and ethical behavior within the workplace.

Policy

  • Compliance with Applicable Laws
    • All employees are required to comprehend and adhere to environmental, safety, and fair dealing laws. While performing job duties and engaging with the company's products, finances, critical information, and public image, employees are expected to demonstrate ethical and responsible conduct. When uncertain about the permissibility of an action under law or Company policy, employees should seek guidance from their resource manager.
  • Respectful Workplace
    • Employees are expected to treat their colleagues with respect, refraining from discriminatory behavior, harassment, or victimization.
  • Protection of Company Property
    • Company property, whether tangible or intangible, must be handled with care and respect.
    • Employees and contractors are required to:
      • Safeguard against the misuse of company equipment.
      • Treat intangible property, such as trademarks, copyright, information, reports, and other materials, with utmost respect, utilizing them solely for job-related tasks.
      • Preserve company facilities and material property from damage and vandalism to the best of their ability.
    • Professional Appearance
  • In the workplace, employees must maintain an appropriate and professional appearance, adhering to the company's dress code.
  • Avoidance of Corruption
    • Employees are discouraged from accepting gifts from clients or partners, and any form of bribery for personal or external benefit is strictly prohibited.
  • Ethical Job Performance and Authority
    • Job duties should be executed with integrity and respect for all involved parties. Supervisors and managers are responsible for judiciously exercising their authority, considering competency and workload when assigning tasks to team members. Team members are expected to follow instructions diligently and fulfill their duties thoughtfully and punctually.
  • Punctuality and Attendance
    • Employees are expected to adhere to their designated work schedule, arriving and departing punctually as outlined by their hiring manager. Exceptions may be approved by the hiring manager for valid reasons.
  • Conflict of Interest
    • Employees should refrain from engaging in personal, financial, or other interests that might conflict with their job responsibilities.
  • Collaborative Spirit
    • Employees are encouraged to foster an environment of collaboration and camaraderie with colleagues, while avoiding disruptions or obstacles to their peers' work.
  • Effective Communication
    • Open communication is essential among colleagues, supervisors, and team members, facilitating smooth workflows and informed decision-making.
  • Responsible Benefit Utilization
    • Employees are expected to use employment benefits responsibly, including time off, insurance, facilities, subscriptions, and other offerings provided by the company.
  • Adherence to Company Policies
    • All employees are required to abide by company policies. Any inquiries should be directed to hiring managers and/or Human Resources.
  • Disciplinary Measures
    • Repeated or intentional violations of the Code of Conduct Policy will result in disciplinary actions. Consequences will vary based on the nature of the violation and may include:
    • Demotion
    • Reprimand
    • Suspension or Termination
    • Temporarily or Indefinitely Revoked Benefits
    • Cases involving corruption, theft, embezzlement, or other unlawful behavior may lead to legal actions.
