Purpose and Scope

  • This policy outlines the framework for recovering Information Technology (IT) infrastructure and services swiftly and efficiently in the event of a disaster or disruptive incident.
  • The policy aims to meet a set Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and encompasses all resources and processes required for data and service restoration.
  • It covers all aspects of information security within the domain of business continuity management.

Background

  • This policy defines the organization's overarching strategy for disaster recovery.
  • The strategy entails defining the RTO, which stipulates the timeframe and service levels for restoring critical business processes post-disaster.
  • The policy outlines the procedures, responsibilities, and technical guidance to fulfill the RTO. Additionally, the policy provides contact details for personnel and service providers relevant to disaster recovery.

Policy

  • This policy prescribes the actions and principles governing disaster recovery:

  • Critical Services, Key Tasks, and SLAs:

  • Key tasks and Service Level Agreements (SLAs) must adhere to the organization's objectives, agreements, and legal, contractual, or regulatory commitments: [list key tasks and SLAs to be maintained during disaster recovery, with respective deadlines]
  • The organization's Recovery Time Objective (RTO) is set at [specify maximum allowable time for critical processes restoration, encompassing relocation and resumption of critical services/technologies]. Relocation and reinstatement of critical services and technologies should be accomplished within this timeframe.
  • Notification of Plan Initiation:
    • The following personnel must be informed when this plan is activated: [list personnel titles requiring notification upon plan activation] [Title of person responsible for notifications] is tasked with notifying the above-listed personnel.
  • Plan Deactivation:
    • The plan may only be deactivated by [identify individuals authorized to deactivate the plan, including job titles].

    • The organization is committed to swiftly restoring normal business operations.
